Meet Tom

Tom Blalock (E. Thomas Blalock II) was born in South Boston, Virginia and moved to the City of Fredericksburg in August 1967 as his Father, Earl T. Blalock was hired as the Principal of Maury Elementary. His Mother (Bobbie M. Blalock) was a life-long English Teacher, teaching English at Gayle Middle School and Stafford High School for 35 years. Tom is the youngest of 3 siblings and very thankful to have 2 wonderful sisters; Susan Blalock Mann of Richmond and Mignon Blalock Osmer of Long Island, New York. Tom and his Family lived on Kenmore Avenue for one year and moved ot the Confederate Ridge Subdivision in 1968, where his Mother still resides. His Father is deceased, 8 May 2004.

Tom grew up in the City of Fredericksburg attending: Hugh Mercer Elementary, Maury elementary, Walker-Grant Middle and James Monroe High School, though he graduated from Courtland High School. Tom continued his life-time of learning with educational achievements, to include: University of Mary Washington with a Bachelor of Science, Troy University with an Executive Masters in Business Administration. Additionally, receiving certificates from: School of Bank Management from the University of Virginia, Graduate School of Banking at Louisiana State University and the Virginia Bankers Association Leadership Institute at Virginia Commonwealth University.

Career

Tom began his Banking Career on 23 December 1985 as a part-time teller at First Virginia Bank in the Spotsylvania Mall, while attending the University of Mary Washington. Upon Graduation from UMW, Tom was accepted to the First Virginia Bank's Management Training Program on 2 January 1990. Tom spent the first eight years in Retail Banking/Branch Office Administration and transitioning to the Commercial Division for the remainder of his career escalating to a Senior Vice President and Commercial relationship Officer with Union Bank & Trust Company.

Tom began the most rewarding and challenging position in his professional career on 2 January 2011 serving as the Chief Financial Officer of the Radley Automotive Group, Inc. that includes: Radley Chevrolet, Radley Cadillac, Radley Collision Centers (Fredericksburg and Stafford), Karen Radley Acura, Karen Radley VW and Radley Acura. Thank you to Karen and Vincent Radley for the opportunity to advance my critical thinking skills as an Executive Leader at the Radley Automotive Group, Inc. and the automotive industry.

Community

Tom has been actively involved in the Community, to include, but not limited to: Fredericksburg Rotary Club (22years), Germanna Community College Educational Foundation (14 years), Community Foundation of the Rappahannock River Region (6 years), Fredericksburg Jaycees (17 years), Rappahannock United Way, Fredericksburg Regional Chamber of commerce and St. Georges Episcopal Church. Additionally, Tom has been engaged in the Virginia Bankers Association and the Virginia Automobile Dealers Association.

Tom has been a member of the Fredericksburg Rotary Club (Organized May 1922) for 22 years, serving as President for Fiscal-Year 2005-2006 and this role was a high-light in my life and the Rotary Organization has made a profound impact in my life. The Rotary Organization mission is "Service Above Self" and The Four-Way Test is a non-partisan and non-sectarian ethical guide for Rotarians to use for their personal and professional relationships. Of the things we think, say or do:

  1. Is it the TRUTH?
  2. Is it FAIR to all concerned?
  3. Will it build GOODWILL and BETTER FRIENDSHIPS?
  4. Will it be BENEFICIAL to all concerned?

Personal Life

Tom and Lauren were married on 17 April 2004 at St. Georges Episcopal Church and are blessed with three wonderful children: Date, Trip and Connor. Lauren is an accomplished and successful Executive with Mary Washington Healthcare as a Vice President of Regulatory Affairs and Enterprise Risk Management. We enjoy Family activities that include: Fredericksburg Parks and Recreation, Fredericksburg Field House, Fredericksburg Ballet Center, Upward Basketball and the Fredericksburg Country Club. Activities include: football, basketball, baseball, ballet, swimming, tennis, golf and a Family game of corn hole. The Blalock Family lives each day with purpose: God/Family/Career...Work/Life/Balance.

Tom would like to ask for your support and most importantly, your VOTE on 7 November 2017 so he may begin to serve the citizens and businesses of the City of Fredericksburg. Upon his election, Tom will make a commitment to the City of Fredericksburg for the remaining 20 years in my career; providing fiscal responsibility, striving for efficiency in government and promoting the City of Fredericksburg as the best place to live, work, worship, play and raise a Family.
